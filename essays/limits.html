Limits

September 2025 • 1 min read

Limits have a negative cultural connotation since we see them as the contrary to our willpower, but how many times have we heard that saying is more important than saying yes? And yet it seems we are constantly judging ourselves for missing shots or opportunities.

But this is not true, limits are there for a reason. There's a possibility we can do anything we want, but not everything and I genuinely believe limits are the best path to doing so. So maybe we should go easy on the conception we have of them.

It is a real irony that we have given so much value to the concept of saying yes to everything, as if we were going to run out of options. But if we don't we start saying no soon, there's a higher possibility of really missing out on an opportunity to shape ourselves and our lives.
