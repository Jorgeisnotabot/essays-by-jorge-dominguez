Discipline

September 2025 • 1 min read

Is discipline a virtue? or is it our own prison? I mean, Have we ever thought Why are we so obsessed with perfection? Why should we strive at everything we do? Why do we fear failure? and Why are we constantly monitoring ourselves to avoid shame?

It's quite easy to blame our families but this goes beyond that, it's a cultural thing or perhaps an institutional thing. So maybe we should reflect on our own actions and realize that we alone are the ones who are judging ourselves not others.
